About me

My name is Alex Hansen and I'm currently studying game programming at The Game Assembly in Stockholm Sweden. I'm a very flexible programmer and enjoy most areas, but my favorites have got to be gameplay, UI and Tools.

During my time at The Game Assembly I have worked on 7(soon to be 8) game projects where I have gotten the opperutnity to experience most parts of the game making process programming wise. I have also realised that I really like working with other disciplines and find satisfaction in making their workflow easier.

I'm currently searching for an internship position at a game studio to finish my education and to hopefully stay afterwards!
