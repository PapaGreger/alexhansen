About me

My name is Alex Hansen and I'm currently studying game programming at The Game Assembly in Stockholm Sweden. I'm a very flexible programmer and enjoy most areas, but my favorites have got to be gameplay, UI and Tools.

During my time at The Game Assembly I have worked on 8 group game projects where I have gotten the oppertunity to experience most parts of the game making process programming wise. I'm currently doing an internship as the final part of my studies and am open to future job opportunities
